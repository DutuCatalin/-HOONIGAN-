Hanging out with Mario Andretti

I love shooting all forms of racing whether it be drag racing or time attack. Every year when IndyCar comes into town at the Long Beach Grand Prix I make a point to go check it out and shoot open wheel racing. It's my yearly fix of some of the greatest racing in the world. This year my friends at Magnaflow offered to bring me out to Pocono to check out the home race for the Andretti racing family. Specifically my assignment was to follow and photography a day in the life of Mario Andretti.
