Wekfest Los Angeles 2018: The best of Southern California

When we're not in the Donut Garage building cars, attempting man line or setting things on fire, we like check out the local car scene. If we'd have to guess, Wekfest is still one of the raddest car shows to ever come to Los Angeles. No joke, many of these cars belong on our Build Biology show. It's unlikely that you'd find something like our shart kart at this show since the Wekfest staff personally curates what type of cars make it in the showroom floor. That just means we're left with the dopest cars Southern California has to offer. Enjoy these photos from our FNG Jofel.
